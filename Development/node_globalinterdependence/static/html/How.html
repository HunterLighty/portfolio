How Does Global Interdependence Impact Me?

Consumer Goods

For starters, you and I have the ability to experience things our forefathers could only have dreamed of! Every product we touch is connected to international trade in most cases. That cell phone? It's components are from Asia. Those fruits you enjoy? Many come from South America. These connections exist in ways we take for granted, a good exercise to explore what life would be like without the benefits of Global Interdependence is to try and find all the items in your room or house that are entirely domestically produced.

Employment

Our jobs, be it producing goods or providing services, can often lead us to interacting with foreign clients. This is just one way in which we interact with the system of Global Interdependence. Think about where the products or services you contribute to end up, and think about the impacts your job has on the people living in those places. Without Global Interdependence, you aswell as others would be limited to domestic services and goods, which can often be of a lower quality, more expensive, or both.

Education

The need to trade and interact with the global community also causes another interesting effect. Children of this modern system are heavily encouraged to learn about other cultures, religions, and most importantly languages. Spanish and English in the West are particularly prominent languages to know for interacting with clients in the United States and South America. This of course helps us broaden our horizons and absorb different cultures, while also meeting new people we otherwise wouldn't have paid any mind to due to the language barriers involved.

One System For All Nations!

So you see, Global Interdependence has affected most of our lives from the day we were born.
